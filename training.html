Qualification and training

Rehabilitation & Trainning

The selection includes recruitment and presentation of rehabilitation and training programs, training and employment, and since the nature of the activity is service, the nature of work basically depends on many axes as factors for success before recruitment and after recruitment

Rehabilitation Before Recruitment

Especially for new workers who have not worked before in any of the
Gulf countries, we offer

Rehabilitation courses and specialized workshops through specialized institutes for the rehabilitation of workers

Connecting and building bridges between our partners and rehabilitation, training and employment centers

Suggesting rehabilitation programs, identifying needs, and preparing and setting up rehabilitation programs

Preparing a plan for attracting, selecting, training and abroad, and providing temporal and spatial service

Rehabilitation After Recruitment

After the worker arrives to Saudi Arabia, our role is to rehabilitate the
workers at home through

Re-introducing the qualification programs, orientations and workshops for the workforce

Provide a calm environment to help him understand and guide Saudi culture

Providing them with knowledge and experience and providing them with the necessary tools

Contribute to the work of preparing educational aids, including brochures and various media

Providing an integrated package of rehabilitation services, whether direct or indirect
